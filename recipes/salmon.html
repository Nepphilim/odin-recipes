Salmon recipe

Description

Fried salmon with fried potatoes is a simple and quick to make dish which requires just the basics found in almost every kitchen. Preseason the salmon, fry chopped potatoes on some oil adding parsley and butter for the final crisp once you get started on frying the salmon.

Ingredients

  • salmon
  • potatoes
  • parsley
  • butter
  • oil
  • vegetables

Steps

  1. Unfrost and season the salmon
  2. Peel and slice potatoes
  3. Fry potatoes in a pan on some oil, add some salt
  4. When potatoes are slowly turning golden, start frying the salmon seperately, at the same time add butter and chopped parsley to the potato
  5. When everything else is almost done quickly fry some vegetables like zucchini on a bit of oil
